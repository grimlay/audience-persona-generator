# 🎯 Audience Persona Generator

A system for building detailed, marketing-ready audience personas using AI — built for fintech marketers who need to move beyond generic audience assumptions and write copy that actually resonates.

Each folder contains three things:
1. **A blank template** — fill in manually when you have research or data to work from
2. **An AI prompt** — paste into Claude with minimal input to generate a full persona in under 60 seconds
3. **An example output** — a completed persona so you can see what good looks like before you run the prompt yourself

---

## Why this exists

Most audience personas stop at job title and company size. They don't tell you what keeps someone up at night, what language they use to describe their problems, what would make them click, or what objection they'd raise in a sales call.

This system goes deeper — covering pain points, motivations, content behaviour, channel preferences, buying triggers, and the specific objections your marketing needs to overcome.

Built from hands-on campaign work across B2B fintech events and consumer financial services in APAC.

---

## How to use it

**Option A — AI-generated (fastest)**
1. Open the `prompt.md` file for your vertical
2. Paste the prompt into [Claude](https://claude.ai)
3. Fill in the input fields with your product and audience details
4. Get a full persona in under 60 seconds
5. Review and refine based on what you know from real customer conversations

**Option B — Manual (most accurate)**
1. Open the `template.md` file for your vertical
2. Fill in each section using existing research, CRM data, or customer interviews
3. Use the example output as a benchmark for the level of detail to aim for

## Persona dimensions covered

Every persona in this system covers four dimensions:

- **Role & seniority** — who they are professionally and where they sit in the organisation
- **Pain points & motivations** — what frustrates them, what they're trying to achieve, what success looks like
- **Content & channel behaviour** — where they consume information, what formats they trust, who influences them
- **Buying triggers & objections** — what moves them to act and what makes them hesitate
